About NeuroCharge

NeuroCharge Medical is developing a handheld neuro-support platform that captures heart-rate-variability and electrodermal activity to detect rising stress and cognitive load. Users receive real-time, physiology-matched support. Clinicians gain insight into real-world patterns to support safe transitions and informed medication review.
