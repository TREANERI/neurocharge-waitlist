About NeuroCharge

NeuroCharge Medical is developing a handheld, EEG-integrated device that monitors neural activity and supports adaptive dosing for personalised neuro-care. Designed to enhance mental-health management, improve medication response, and reduce hospital pressure through remote monitoring.

Real-Time Neuro Monitoring

Collects EEG data at the lips to track user states and provide clinicians with actionable insights.

Adaptive Dosing

AI-driven algorithms adjust therapy guidance based on physiological feedback, supporting precision and safety.

Hospital-to-Community Impact

Enables early intervention, reduces readmissions, and supports data-driven care across home and clinical settings.
